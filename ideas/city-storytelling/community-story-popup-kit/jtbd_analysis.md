# Jobs To Be Done Analysis

## Job Type

Functional

## Situation

When mobile pop-ups are scheduled at churches and senior centers in Richmond’s East End or Southside

## Persona

City/community engagement staffer

## Motivation

capture elders’ place-based stories and photos offline with consistent prompts and consent

## Outcome

convert them into actionable civic insights aligned with Richmond 300 priorities and share back with residents

## Current Workaround

Ad hoc listening sessions, paper comment cards, scattered survey links, and occasional partner-led oral histories without a standard intake or metadata model.

## Core Pain

Fragmented data, inaccessible audio files, weak metadata (location, topic, consent), and little feedback loop into City planning/Capital Improvement decisions.


# Open Questions

## Category

Data

## Question Text

What consent language will we use to cover offline capture, local archiving, public sharing, and potential City use in planning reports while offering opt-in tiers? (Noting VCDPA exemptions but ethical best practices.)
