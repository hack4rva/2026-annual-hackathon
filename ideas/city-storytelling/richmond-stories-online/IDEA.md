# Richmond Stories Online

**Project:** Richmond Stories Online
**Pillar:** Pillar 7 — A City That Tells Its Stories
**Partners:** The Valentine Museum, Civic Hackers, Local Storytellers
**Hackathon:** Hack for RVA (Track 7)
**Website:** RichmondStories.online

---

## Vision

> "Every city has a soul, but in Richmond, our soul is woven from centuries of complex, powerful narratives. For too long, these stories have been fragmented, tucked away in inaccessible archives or whispered in forgotten corners."

Richmond Stories Online is a **digital sanctuary for Richmond's collective memory** — a living, growing archive that uses advanced geospatial mapping to turn every street corner into a gateway to hidden history.

---

## What It Is

Richmond Stories Online is a platform for:

- **Oral histories** that preserve the lived experiences of Richmond's neighbors and communities
- **Geospatial mapping** that anchors stories to the physical places where they happened
- **Historical preservation** with a direct focus on confronting Richmond's colonial history and the socioeconomic legacies that persist today
- **Crowdsourced contribution** — residents can upload their own photos and narratives today

The archive is explicitly described as **living and growing**, not a static collection.

---

## Hackathon Context (Track 7 Work)

Within Track 7 at Hack4RVA, technologists and advocates came together to solve the challenge of **digital historical preservation**. Specific work included:

- Building **APIs to sync local archives**, unifying fragmented city records into one accessible hub
- Exploring **augmented reality** to bring history to life at the exact locations where it occurred
- Making **discovery intuitive** — turning a search bar into a journey through time

---

## Core Features

### Geospatial Mapping
- Every street corner in Richmond can become a gateway to a piece of hidden history
- Users can navigate the city through a map interface anchored to real historical moments and locations

### Oral History Integration
- Preserves firsthand accounts from community members
- Captures the "heart of the community" in the voices of the people who lived it

### Curated Story Paths
- Guided paths that lead users through threads of Richmond's identity
- Designed to make discovery intuitive rather than requiring users to already know what they're looking for

### Crowdsourced Contributions
- Any resident can contribute their own photos and narratives
- The goal is to give everyone — past and present — a platform to be heard

---

## Why It Matters

### Social Equity
> "To move forward, we must face our challenging colonial history and the socioeconomic legacies that remain today. Preserving these narratives is more than archiving — it's building a legacy of truth and social equity."

The project uses technology explicitly as a **tool for social equity**, ensuring every voice is represented and accessible.

### Economic Impact
The platform is also framed as a driver of **tourism and economic growth** — by telling authentic stories and celebrating Richmond's diversity, the project invites the world to discover the real Richmond, which in turn revitalizes neighborhoods while honoring their history.

### Diversity as Strength
> "Our diversity isn't just a fact, it's our greatest strength and our most vital story."
