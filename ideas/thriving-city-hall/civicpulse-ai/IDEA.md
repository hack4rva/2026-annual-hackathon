# CivicPulse AI — Smart Procurement Intelligence Hub

**Team:** Civic Pulse AI — Bhaskaran Jairajan, Prasanna Partiban, Vardarajan Seduraman
**Pillar:** Thriving City Hall — Procurement Intelligence
**Hackathon:** Hack for RVA

---

## Problem Statement

Procurement teams in city government currently spend hours **manually reviewing contracts**: digging through PDFs, tracking expiration dates, and identifying risks. This manual approach leads to:

- **Missed renewal deadlines**
- **Inefficient manual effort** duplicated across staff
- **Limited visibility** into upcoming contract decisions
- **Critical insights buried inside documents** — never surfaced until it's too late

---

## The Core Question

> "What if AI could turn contracts into actionable intelligence instantly?"

That's exactly what the team built.

---

## Solution: SmartGo Procurement Intelligence Hub

A two-component AI platform that transforms raw contract documents into structured, actionable intelligence for city procurement staff.

---

## Component 1: Contract Pulse View Dashboard

The **Contract Pulse View** is a real-time situational awareness tool for procurement staff.

### What It Shows
- **Upcoming contract expirations** — with precise timelines
- **Renewable windows** — so staff know when action must be taken
- **Urgency levels** — prioritized view of what needs attention now vs. later
- **A real-time snapshot** of the entire procurement workload

### Intelligence Layer
Beyond display, the dashboard actively:
- **Flags contracts** based on approaching deadlines
- **Flags contracts** based on risk indicators
- Functions as an **early warning system** for decisions before they become emergencies

---

## Component 2: Document Insight Extractor

The **Document Insight Extractor** is the AI engine that processes raw contract PDFs.

### Interface
- Two-tile layout: extraction results on the left, **document preview** on the right
- Staff can see exactly what the AI pulled and verify it against the source document simultaneously

### What the AI Extracts
From any uploaded contract PDF, the system automatically pulls:
- **Expiration dates**
- **Contract values**
- **Supplier/vendor details**
- **Key contractual terms**
- **Pricing language**

### Missing Data Detection
The system also **flags missing data** — if a contract lacks an expiration date or key field, the AI calls it out explicitly rather than silently ignoring it.

---

## Impact

With this intelligence hub, city procurement staff gain:

| Before | After |
|---|---|
| Hours scanning PDFs manually | Key details extracted automatically in seconds |
| Risk of missed renewal deadlines | Early warning system with urgency flags |
| Staff buried in document review | Staff focused on decisions, not data gathering |
