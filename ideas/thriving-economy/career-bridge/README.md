# Career Bridge

**Pillar:** A Thriving Economy
**Problem Statement:** Help first-time entrepreneurs understand what steps to take, in what order, to start and register a business in Richmond.
**Source:** Demo
**Team:** River City Hackers — Jake Yazbek

JTBD analysis in this pillar folder frames the idea next to small-business navigation; the product itself (career matching, barriers, employment pathways) aligns more closely with **Thriving Families — Youth Employment Pathways** than with pure business registration.
