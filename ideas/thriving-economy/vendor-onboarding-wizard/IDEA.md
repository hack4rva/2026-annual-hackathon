# Vendor Onboarding Wizard

**Source:** NEW  
**Pillar:** A Thriving Economy  
**Problem Statement:** Help minority-owned businesses identify and understand City contracting opportunities that match their capabilities.

---

## Description

**Core assumption challenged:** Vendor onboarding friction is purely a technology problem; the analysis frames it as **process fragmentation** across federal (SAM.gov), state (eVA), and local layers that no single stack fully integrates in a weekend. First-time Richmond vendors face weeks of delay and high abandonment, especially MBEs without compliance staff, with no unified guide. The innovation combines **eliminating duplicate data entry** across steps (pre-population concept), **reducing document ambiguity** (clear waits and requirements, e.g., CAGE timelines), **raising status visibility** (Draft / ready / filed across jurisdictions), and **creating a registration readiness score** so vendors see percent-complete path to bid eligibility.

## Key Approach

- Intake questionnaire → personalized roadmap: which systems to use, in what order, with document lists and estimated waits.
- Progress tracker across SAM.gov, eVA, and City registration (MVP may simulate integrations; real APIs are a later phase).
- Stretch: “what you can bid on now” based on current completion state; APEX Accelerator or clinic finder hooks for human help.
- MBE path: eligibility and certification guidance where OMBD rules are documented without API access.
- Pair digital roadmap with human support channels emphasized in research (training volume and outcomes from accelerator networks).
