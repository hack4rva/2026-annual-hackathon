# First Job SMS Kit

**Source:** NEW  
**Pillar:** Thriving Families  
**Problem Statement:** Give Richmond youth one clear place to explore summer jobs, internships, and first-job guidance.

---

## Description

An **SMS-based toolkit** that guides Richmond teens through first-job readiness — labor law basics, document checklists (work permit, SSN, photo ID), interview prep micro-drills, and shift-day reminders — over text, with no app download and no broadband requirement, to reach teens who lack reliable home computer access. **Core assumption challenged:** teens need an app or website; evidence cited includes strong lifts from adding SMS to email-only workflows and no-app chatbot reach at scale. Richmond teens lose offers or fail to start because of missing documents, unknown rights, and missed orientation deadlines while existing resources assume web, apps, or in-person attendance.

## Key Approach

- TCPA-compliant opt-in at program enrollment; branching SMS sequence (e.g., Twilio Studio) over roughly two weeks: documents, permit steps, interview practice, reminders.
- Virginia-specific labor-law snippets vetted by counsel; optional Spanish track and parent/guardian parallel thread for signature bottlenecks.
- Optional transit-aware first-day reminder if schedule data is available; escalation from bot to human for edge cases.
- Measure document completion, orientation attendance, and comprehension vs. control groups where programs allow.
- Low-cost per-message economics at small cohort scale as described in the analysis.
