# Multi-Pillar AI Platform

**Presenter:** Subhash Krishnamurthy
**Scope:** All 7 Mayoral Action Plan Pillars
**Hosting:** Google Cloud
**Hackathon:** Hack for RVA

---

## Overview

Subhash built a single AI platform hosted on Google Cloud that addresses **all seven pillars** of the Mayoral Action Plan simultaneously. The platform uses multiple AI agents working in concert and pulls live data from a variety of sources across each pillar area.

---

## Thriving Families — Career / Job Market Module

Focused on youth employment, particularly 14–15 year olds entering the job market for the first time.

### Flow
1. **Registration** — Users can register or, if their name is already in the system, they can skip registration and be recognized directly
2. **Opportunity Discovery** — Browse available job opportunities in the system
3. **Preparation** — The core work of the module happens here; this is described as where the maximum work is done for the candidate

### AI Coach Features (within Preparation)
Once a candidate selects a specific job:
- **Skill analysis** — The AI coach evaluates the candidate's current skills
- **Gap analysis** — Identifies what skills are missing relative to the target job's requirements
- Works from the candidate's **resume** (if provided) or from a general profile
- Lists what is required for the specific job alongside what the candidate currently has, so they can prepare specifically

### Transportation Routing
After skill prep, the platform also addresses **getting there** — providing transportation routes to the job location, including all available options.

### Video Conversation with AI Agent
The presenter describes this as "the best part": candidates can have a **real-time video conversation** with the AI agent:
- The agent helps them navigate various aspects of the job search and preparation process
- Users can talk to the agent in real time and receive real-time feedback
- Functions like a live coaching session

### ATS Resume Builder
The platform generates a full **ATS-ready resume** for the candidate — formatted to pass Applicant Tracking Systems used by employers.

## A City That Tells Its Stories — Live Events & Story Hubs

### Live Events
- Pulls live event data from **Ticketmaster**
- Displays events happening in Richmond in real time for residents and visitors

### Story Hubs
- A **mobile-friendly platform** where community members can upload pictures, stories, and memories
- Examples of content: grandmother's recipes, neighborhood history, community photos
- Designed to be a living repository of community culture and stories
