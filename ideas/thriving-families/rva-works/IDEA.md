# RVA Works — Youth Internship Portal

**Presenter:** Parthav (college sophomore)
**Team:** Eco Warriors
**Pillar:** Thriving Families
**Hackathon:** Hack for RVA

---

## Problem Statement

> "As a sophomore in college, I'm aspiring to become a part of the workforce. I often find myself lost trying to find internships."

Young people in Richmond — particularly teens — face significant friction when trying to enter the workforce. Finding available internships is difficult, the application process is confusing, and the documentation requirements are unclear. Employers, meanwhile, need a verified way to post opportunities specifically for teen workers while remaining compliant with Virginia youth employment law.

---

## Solution

**RVA Works** is a two-sided portal — one for **teens** seeking internships and one for **employers** seeking to hire them — with AI working in the background to power both sides.

---

## Teen Portal

### Browse Internships
- Teens can browse all available internship opportunities listed in the portal
- Each listing shows key information:
  - **Pay rate**
  - **Schedule**
  - **Transit time from the nearest bus stop** — a critical practical detail for teens without cars

### Document Checklist
- Each internship includes a **document checklist** that the teen can follow
- Walks them through exactly which documents need to be gathered and submitted to apply for that specific job

### First Job Kit — A Standout Feature
The **First Job Kit** is a comprehensive, step-by-step guide designed for teens who have never applied for a job before. It includes:

- **Message scripts** — pre-written templates for how to reach out about a job posting
- **Step-by-step application guides** — exactly how to apply for different roles, permissions, and programs
- **Guardian signature guidance** — how to get the parental/guardian approvals required for youth employment
- Answers to every common question a first-time job applicant would have

The First Job Kit is positioned as the key resource for a teen who has no prior experience navigating the employment system.

---

## Employer Portal

### Account Creation & Verification
- Employers who want to hire teens must **create an account**
- This adds an extra layer of security and verification — ensuring that teen workers are connecting with legitimate employers only

### Job Description Analysis
After posting a job description, employers receive:
- An **automated analysis** of the job posting
- A check against **Virginia Youth Employment Law** — flagging any issues before the posting goes live
- A **detailed Youth Law analysis** report

### Hire-a-Teen Guide
A step-by-step guide for employers who are new to hiring teen workers:
1. Register for the **Dolly portal** (with direct link)
2. Fill out required paperwork (with direct links)
3. Follow required tasks (e.g., establish house rules, required training)
4. Track all necessary compliance steps

---

## Technology

The platform is powered by **intelligent AI in the background** that:
- Gathers and structures information for both teens and employers
- Powers the job posting analysis against Virginia Youth Law
- Supports the guidance and resource-surfacing features throughout the portal
