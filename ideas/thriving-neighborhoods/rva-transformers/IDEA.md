# RVA TRANSFORMERS — Unified City Navigation Platform

**Pillar:** Thriving Neighborhoods
**Two Components:** RVA 311 Bridge + RVA Biz Navigator
**Hackathon:** Hack for RVA

---

## The Core Problem

> "In Richmond today, help exists, opportunity exists — but neither is easy to reach."

A resident in crisis doesn't know where to go. A small business owner doesn't know where to start. Different people, same problem — and it isn't a service gap, it's a **navigation failure**.

The solution: a unified way to navigate the city, from support to opportunity.

---

## Component 1: RVA 311 Bridge — Resident Access

### The Problem It Solves
A resident needs help at midnight. They don't speak English. They don't trust the system. Existing 311 access is constrained by hours, tech requirements, and language.

### What Was Built
A **multilingual, 24/7 interface** connecting residents to real support — without collecting personal data.

### Design Principles
- **Mirrors the existing 311 system** — so it's immediately familiar and doesn't require learning a new mental model
- Keeps existing services intact and **expands access** for underserved populations
- Specifically designed for: immigrants, refugees, residents needing housing, food, and mental health support

### Privacy by Design
- Every interaction begins with a **privacy notice**
- **No personal information is required or stored**
- The system searches trusted city and nonprofit data sources and returns clear guidance with verified sources

### Trusted Source Connections
When appropriate, the system **directly connects residents to a trusted local organization** — closing the loop from information to action.

### Phone Access (No Internet Required)
The same full experience is available **over a basic phone** — in multiple languages — with **no internet connection required**.

### Live Demo Shown
A resident uses the interface:
> *"Hi, I'm a new immigrant living in Richmond City. Where can I go for help?"*

System response:
> *"Welcome. I'm here to help. As a new immigrant in Richmond City, you have several free resources available to you regardless of your immigration status."*

## Component 2: RVA Biz Navigator — Business Mobility

### The Problem It Solves
A first-time entrepreneur wants to start a business but faces:
- Multiple city departments with no clear starting point
- Unclear steps and unfamiliar language
- Confusion about zoning, licensing, and contracting — often spending money or time on steps taken in the wrong order

### What Was Built
**RVA Biz Navigator** — a single starting point that guides business owners step by step through the full process of starting and operating a business in Richmond.

### The Four-Phase Journey
The navigator generates a **personalized journey** across four sequential phases:

1. **Legal Information** — foundational business structure requirements
2. **Zoning and Location** — confirms whether a specific business type is allowed at a specific location *before any money is spent*
3. **Licensing** — step-by-step guidance through required licenses
4. **Contracting** — direct access to real city contract opportunities

All four phases are powered by **live city, state, and federal data**.

### The Outcome
> "What used to take weeks of confusion becomes a clear path forward in minutes."
