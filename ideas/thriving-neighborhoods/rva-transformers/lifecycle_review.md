# Lifecycle Review — RVA TRANSFORMERS

**Pillar (submitted):** Thriving Neighborhoods  
**Demo:** RVA TRANSFORMERS (RVA 311 Bridge + RVA Biz Navigator)  
**Status:** **MISALIGNED**

---

## Summary

The demo bundles two products that belong to **different MAP pillars**. Neither addresses **development discovery** or **housing compliance**.

- **RVA 311 Bridge** is a strong candidate for **Thriving City Hall** review: multilingual access, privacy-by-design (no data collected), and phone-based use with no internet — materially expanding how residents reach services.
- **RVA Biz Navigator** is a strong candidate for **Thriving Economy** review: a clear **four-phase** guided path (legal → zoning → licensing → contracting) using live government data addresses first-time business navigation directly.

Neither component engages early visibility into neighborhood development proposals or staff-side affordable housing compliance tracking.

---

## Recommendation

**Cross-reference** this work to **A Thriving City Hall** and **A Thriving Economy** pillar (or award) evaluations. For **Thriving Neighborhoods** lifecycle and rubric alignment, document **misalignment** and do not treat neighborhood problem statements as satisfied by this demo.
