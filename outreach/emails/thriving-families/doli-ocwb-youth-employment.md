**[View Full Research & Questions](https://github.com/hack4rva/2026-annual-hackathon/blob/main/outreach/questions/thriving-families/doli-ocwb-youth-employment.md)**

- **VAeECS is a closed system:** No APIs, deep-linking, or pre-population — each party must use the DOLI portal independently; external tools can only point users there.
- **Fragmented ecosystem:** Richmond runs 7+ parallel youth employment programs with different ages, requirements, and windows — and no shared referral backbone.
- **Foster youth barrier:** Virginia code provides no alternative signer for unaccompanied minors on work permits; guardianship must be established first.
- **Enforcement disparity:** 2018 RPD data (reported by WVTF) showed 98% of juveniles stopped for curfew violations were Black — relevant to late-shift work and youth employment.
