## Body

Hi [First Name],

I'm writing from the RVA Hacks civic hackathon team. We ran RVA Hacks March 27–29, 2026, a civic technology weekend focused on Richmond's Mayor's Action Plan. Several teams built prototypes for the Thriving Families pillar on Youth Employment Pathways — one clear place for Richmond youth to explore summer jobs, internships, and first-job guidance.

We've completed extensive post-event research and have **five** specific questions for your team. We've documented our confirmed findings and detailed questions here:

**[View Full Research & Questions](https://github.com/hack4rva/2026-annual-hackathon/blob/main/outreach/questions/thriving-families/rps-crwp-workforce.md)**

- **RPS / WIOA gap:** CRWP in-school youth contracts went to Peace of Mind and Charles City County — not RPS — so Richmond Public Schools lack a formal, scaled in-school WIOA navigator presence during the school year. *(Source: Capital Region Workforce Partnership FY2024 WIOA performance report; CRWP contract award records, reviewed April 2026)*
- **No summer handoff:** Youth Works RVA is an 8-week program with no structured transition to fall apprenticeships, WIOA WEX/OJT, or other year-round pathways; post-summer churn appears untracked. *(Source: Pillar 3 Working Session notes, March 3, 2026; OCWB Youth Engagement Services program documentation at rva.gov)*
- **Scale under pressure:** CRWP youth "skills gained" fell to 78% in FY24; the FY26 proposed budget shows a 3% decrease — signaling fragility in regional youth workforce funding. *(Source: Capital Region Workforce Partnership FY24 WIOA performance report; Richmond FY26 proposed budget, Workforce Development line items)*
- **Document drop-off:** Boston SuccessLink reports up to 15% of job-matched youth fail to complete onboarding due to permits and documents — a likely parallel risk in Richmond. *(Source: Boston SuccessLink program evaluation data; post-event comparative research, April 2026)*

We're not asking for any commitment — just trying to understand what's technically possible so we can give the Mayor's office a realistic assessment. Happy to share any prototypes or our full research.
