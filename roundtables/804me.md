## Seven Personas (single pass each)

### 1 — INNOVATOR
804Me is not “another map.” The differentiable move is **push + pull + people**: physical QR surfaces → zero-login web → optional SMS/WhatsApp → 211/CBO escalation (per RESEARCH_RESULT and INNOVATION_ANALYSIS). **Navigator profiles** are a real privacy-preserving personalization pattern: self-selected context without retained PII. The ERRC grid is coherent: eliminate accounts and English-first defaults; raise sanctuary-grade privacy and physical presence; create a Richmond-layered stack national directories do not operate. **Risk:** innovation is mostly *distribution and trust design*, not novel algorithms — national Findhelp/211 already cover anonymous browse; differentiation must be *local curation, language quality, and channel fit*.

### 2 — ACTIVIST / BUILDER
**Build outside, partner inside.** A static or JAMstack microsite (Plausible/Matomo, no GA) fits `RICHMOND_TECH_STACK_2026.md` “what a prototype can use.” **Help1RVA/findhelp** has **no free public API** — bulk listing sync is blocked; **Unite Us** is enterprise-only. So the honest MVP is **manual or partner-export curation**, Open Referral–shaped data, or explicit licensing — not silent scraping. Physical “24/7 hubs” are **not** weekend-shippable; treat as **Phase 2** with GRTC/library poster networks (INNOVATION_ANALYSIS Crazy 8s) as the realistic “push” slice. Align with pillar INDEX **H2/H5**: ruthless scope for a Sunday demo.

### 3 — CITIZEN (resident + vulnerable user)
I need to know **who verified each pin** and **when**. If the map is pretty but wrong on hours, eligibility, or “no ID,” I waste a bus fare or miss a meal window — or I stop trusting anything labeled “City-adjacent.” **lifecycle_review.md** is right: without provenance, the anonymity feature does not redeem bad data. I also need **Dari/Pashto/Swahili** paths eventually; EN/ES/AR helps many but not all refugee cohorts INDEX and lifecycle flag.

### 4 — MAYOR / POLITICAL
Politically, **privacy-first service discovery** is aligned with MAP and AR 2.13’s emphasis on minimization and transparency for AI — here, **no AI in the loop** is actually a strength for a first ship. Risk is **liability and optics**: if a listing is wrong or a vendor is not vetted, “City hackathon” framing must not imply **government endorsement** without OIRE/CBO co-brand. Physical infrastructure implies **budget, ADA/kiosk rules, and operations** — the HHS **May 2026** digital accessibility rule (RESEARCH_RESULT) raises the bar for any kiosk-like surface tied to federally assisted programs.

### 5 — ARCHITECT
**Feasible slice:** curated dataset in repo or headless CMS + static map (MapLibre/Leaflet + GeoJSON), i18n with RTL for Arabic, WCAG-oriented UI. **Blocked slice:** automated Help1RVA hydration without partnership. **“Zero data collection”** requires engineering proof: no third-party trackers, minimal server logs, documented retention — lifecycle flags this as unverified in the demo. Provider self-update needs **auth, abuse controls, and moderation** — non-trivial; do not pretend it is “just a tab.”

### 6 — FRONTLINER (CBO / 211-adjacent)
I will only share this with clients if **Sacred Heart, IRC, ReEstablish Richmond**, or similar **visibly co-sign** or “reviewed as of [date].” I already have 211 and personal WhatsApp lists; a new site must **reduce** my explain time, not add another stale directory. Navigator profiles help me **frame** choices without asking for status. **Gap:** no WhatsApp handoff in the demo spec though research says WhatsApp outperforms SMS for engagement (at cost of anonymity).

### 7 — FUNDER
Fund **verification workflow + governance**, not more pins. INNOVATION_ANALYSIS budget bands: translation and messaging are real line items. **Do not fund** bespoke kiosk fleets until partnerships exist. Milestones: (1) published data dictionary + steward org; (2) 25–50 **phone-verified** listings; (3) CBO badge; (4) then SMS/WhatsApp pilot with explicit opt-in.

## Fatal Flaw Test

| Test | Result | Notes |
|------|--------|------|
| **Trust / data integrity** | **Fail (demo as documented)** | Interactive map **without disclosed source, count, or verification** is a **fatal credibility flaw** for immigration-adjacent users (lifecycle_review). |
| **Structural wall (Help1RVA API)** | **Pass for manual MVP** | No API required if listings are **curated/partner-sourced**; **Fail** if the product depends on unsanctioned bulk extract. |
| **Ownership / stewardship** | **Fail (demo as documented)** | No named post-demo data steward → high orphan risk (prior art: most refugee maps died). |
| **Harm potential** | **Elevated** | Wrong shelter hours or eligibility → **material harm**; must ship **verification badges, last-verified date, and escalation to 211**. |
| **Accessibility / kiosk compliance** | **Conditional** | Full kiosk program triggers **WCAG 2.1 AA / equivalent facilitation** expectations (RESEARCH_RESULT); web MVP still should target AA. |

**Fatal flaw (concise):** **Unverified directory + no accountable steward** undermines the entire value proposition for a population whose primary barrier is **trust**, not search UX.

## Verdict

### **DO NOT SHIP AS PUBLIC “PRODUCTION” UNTIL CONDITIONS MET — STRONG PROTOTYPE DIRECTION**

804Me’s **design intent** (no account, no PII, navigator profiles, multilingual, QR bridge) is **among the best-aligned** in the pillar to PS1 and to tech-stack reality **if** content is honest and maintained. As a **demo narrative**, it is **not yet safe to treat as a resident-facing system** without provenance and governance.

### Conditions (minimum)

1. **Publish resource provenance:** count, source, verification method, last-updated field per listing.  
2. **Name a maintaining organization** (CBO or formal coalition) with a **90-day update cadence**.  
3. **Visible trust transfer:** at least one OIRE-listed org co-brand or “reviewed by” panel.  
4. **Privacy audit brief:** third-party scripts, logs, subprocessors — public one-pager.  
5. **Scope honesty:** phase physical hubs; ship **web + posters + 211 escalation copy** first.

## Narrative Summary

804Me encodes the right **ethics** for Thriving and Inclusive Communities PS1: **anonymity as architecture**, **language access**, and **non-digital on-ramps**. Pillar research (INDEX **C1/C5, D1, F2/F3, G1–G3, H2**) and `RICHMOND_TECH_STACK_2026.md` converge on the same constraint: **Richmond’s backbone systems are partner-walled**, so **trustworthy local curation and CBO governance** matter more than feature count. The roundtable’s bottom line is that **without verified data and a named steward**, the product fails the **fatal flaw** test for its intended users; with those conditions satisfied, the same design becomes a **high-leverage** complement to 211 and Help1RVA rather than a fragile duplicate.
